Visualizing the 2022 Wisconsin County Health Report

Food security, housing, and early childhood development are a few examples of important Social Determinants of Health(SDH). SDH are conditions in which people live learn, work, and play that influence health outcomes and risks in both positive and negative ways.

The 2022 Wisconsin County Health Ranking Report highlighted access to housing, childcare, and food as key health equity concerns across Wisconsin Counties. Below you can compare 2022 Wisconsin County data quantifying those concerns.
